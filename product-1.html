Advance Adult Large Oodles Dry Dog Food

ADVANCE dog food formulas deliver optimal nutrition for Australian pets using scientific knowledge and over 45 years of manufacturing experience. ADVANCE products are uniquely formulated for Australian pets.

This super premium, Australian made, dry dog food is complete and balanced to support the health and wellbeing of your large adult Poodle mix. It includes green lipped mussel powder to help support healthy joints and a unique blend of nutrients including taurine and arginine to promote improved cardiac function. Real salmon is the first ingredient, which delivers a high quality source of lean protein and omega fatty acids.

Key Features

  • Super premium, Australian made, dry dog food with salmon as the #1 ingredient
  • Designed specifically for large dogs with Poodle in their breed mix including Labradoodles, Cavoodles, Groodles, etc.
  • No added wheat, whole corn soy & beef
  • Heart Health Nutrients: Supports vascular health and cardiac function
  • Fermentable Prebiotic Fibre: supports balanced gut microbiome
  • Green lipped mussel powder for healthy joints
  • Antioxidants to support a strong immune system
  • Omega 3 and 6 plus zinc for healthy skin and reduced allergy symptoms
  • Controlled mineral levels and high digestibility to support urinary tract health
  • Designed to reduce dental tartar formation
